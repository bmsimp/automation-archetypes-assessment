## Individual Assessment Workflow:
1. Open the **Individual Assessment** tab.
1. Enter the name and role of the person being assessed (e.g. “Jane Smith, Operations Lead”).
1. Score their capability 0–10 on each archetype. Expand each section to read the sub-archetype descriptions and ask: *does this person demonstrate these characteristics?*.
1. Click **+ Add to Team Assessment** to include this person in the team view.

## Interpreting Individual Results
1. Review the live radar chart and total score as you go — they update instantly
1. The **Score Summary** will provide you with a total of their score along with a capability profile from the [Scoring Reference](Scoring-Reference.md).

## Saving Individual Results for Reuse
1. Click **Export** to save the assessment as a `.json` file

## Importing Prior Individual Results
1. Click **Import**
1. Locate your `.json` file and open.
1. Click **+ Add to Team Assessment** to include them in the team view.
