## Team Assessment workflow:
1. Collect individual assessments from each team member
1. Each person completes their own assessment on the Individual Assessment tab and clicks **+ Add to Team Assessment**
1. Or share exported `.json` files with a facilitator who imports them via the Individual Assessment tab

## Interpreting Team Results
1. Where radars diverge on an archetype, that disagreement is worth discussing.
1. Where all evaluators score an archetype low, that is your most urgent gap to address through hiring, training, or partnerships.
1. The archetype with the lowest combined team score represents your most urgent investment area.
1. The totals for each archetype are displayed in the Gap Analysis card from lowest to highest score.

## Saving & Importing Team Results
1. Click Export on the Team Assessment tab to save the full team dataset as a .json file.
1. Click Import to reload a previously exported team assessment.

## Creating a PDF Report
1. Click **Generate Report** on the Team Assessment tab.
1. Choose whether to include individual breakdowns (each person’s scores, radar chart, and per-dimension narrative on separate pages).
1. Choose explainer depth: **Short** (2–3 sentence narrative per dimension) or **Long** (full sub-archetype breakdown with framework citations).
1. Click **Generate Report** to produce a printable PDF of the full team capability profile.
